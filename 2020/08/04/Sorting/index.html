Summary

we know that selection sort, quick sort, bubble sort, insertion sort, merge sort, shell sort, and heap sort are all based on swapping! so swap can turn our unsorted array to some sorted array.

Or not only sorted array, but meet some requirements

Tricks:

swap often comes with two pointers, with one pointer going through the array, the other pointer recording the position for one boundary.

Or swap are often related with nums[nums[i]], the number value is related with nums index. so we can swap num to their correct position and then find the missing one / repeated one.
